# HIPAA amendment workflow anchor (45 CFR 164.526)

Use this module to keep language compliant and avoid overclaims.

## Operational rules to encode

- A patient can request amendment of PHI in a designated record set while the information is maintained.
- The covered entity must act within 60 days of receipt.
- One extension of up to 30 additional days is allowed with a written delay statement sent within the original 60-day window.
- A request may be denied under allowed grounds (for example: not part of the designated record set, not created by the entity in applicable situations, not available for inspection, or record considered accurate and complete).
- If accepted, the amendment must be linked/appended to affected records.
- If denied, response must be written and include:
  - basis for denial
  - right to submit statement of disagreement
  - complaint information
  - future disclosure handling

## Language constraints

- Do say: "The provider must respond on timeline."
- Do not say: "The provider must accept your amendment."
- Keep wording neutral and factual. Avoid legal conclusions.
